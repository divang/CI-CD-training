DevOps Life Cycle
> DevOps is a software engineering culture that unites 
the development and operations team under an umbrella 
of tools to automate evry stage.

> DevOps Phases
> Plan (git, Jira Software)
> Code (GitLab, GitHub)
> Build (maven, gradle)
> Test (cucumber, juint, selenium)
> Integrate (jenkins)
> Deploy (bmc)
> Operate (chef, ansible, puppet, docker)
> Monitor (Grafana, Wireshark)
